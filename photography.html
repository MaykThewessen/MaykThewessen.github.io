How I shoot

Stills on the Canon body, aerial on DJI for clean cinematic moves, FPV builds for the shots a stabilised gimbal can't get — proximity flying through structures, dive lines, racing-line chase. Six different cameras, one workflow.

The point is the place and the moment, not the kit. But the kit shapes what's possible, so I keep a working set rather than a collection.
